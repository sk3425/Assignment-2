Assessing Public Transportation Inequities for Affordable Housing in NYC

This map provides an overview of the unequal access to subway and bus networks in relation to availablity of affordable housing units across New York City. It highlights significant transit gaps, particularly in parts of Queens and Brooklyn, where limited subway connectivity creates transit deserts in South Queens and East Brooklyn.
